About the Game

Midnight Menu combines relaxing restaurant management with supernatural storytelling. During each shift, players prepare recipes, upgrade the diner, and interact with mysterious visitors whose requests become increasingly unusual. It's all about that weird feeling when something cozy feels just a little... off.

Gameplay Experience

Relaxing management with strange encounters

  • Run the Night Shift

    Prepare meals, organize orders, and keep customers satisfied during increasingly busy nights.

  • Discover Secret Recipes

    Collect rare ingredients and unlock unusual menu items tied to supernatural visitors.

  • Upgrade the Diner

    Expand kitchen areas, redesign interiors, and improve service equipment.

  • Meet Unusual Guests

    Every visitor has distinct behavior, dialogue, and hidden lore connected to the world outside thediner.

  • Uncover the Mystery

    Small narrative fragments gradually reveal why the diner only opens after midnight.

Features

We've built everything around creating the right mood and memorable characters

  • Stylized Cozy Horror. A balance of warm visuals, rain-soaked environments, and subtle supernatural tension.

  • Interactive Cooking Systems. Players prepare dishes through quick but satisfying gameplay interactions.

  • Every customer's got their own weird story. Guests return over time with evolving stories and requests.

  • The whole place changes as the night goes on. Weather, lighting, and music shift throughout gameplay sessions.
